FAQ

Common Questions

Why should I buy the Personal Edition instead of using the free version?

The free version is intended for compatibility testing. Personal Edition removes the export limit and includes auto-resume processing for large Google Drive datasets.

Does my data leave my Google account?

No. Everything runs inside Google Apps Script and Google Sheets. No files are uploaded to external servers.

Can I use it with large Google Drives?

Yes. The Personal Edition has been tested with datasets containing more than 50,000 files and folders.

Can I try it before purchasing?

Yes. We strongly recommend trying the Free Trial first to confirm compatibility with your Google account and environment.
