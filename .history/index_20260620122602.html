FAQ

Common Questions

Is this a cloud service?

No. Everything runs inside your Google account using Google Apps Script and Google Sheets. Nothing is hosted externally.

Do my files leave Google Drive?

No. No external server is involved. All processing happens within your own Google account.

Does this work with Shared Drives?

Yes — if your account has the appropriate access permissions to the shared drive or folder.

Can I scan my entire My Drive?

Yes. You can point it at any folder URL, including the root of your My Drive.

Can I use this for Dropbox or OneDrive?

No. This product only supports Google Drive URLs. It is purpose-built for Google Workspace.

Do I need a paid Google Workspace plan?

No. It works with standard Google accounts, subject to standard Google Apps Script execution limits.
