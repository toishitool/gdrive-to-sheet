Privacy Policy

GDrive To Sheet · Last updated: —
🔒

Thank you for using GDrive To Sheet. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we do — and do not — collect, and how the product operates with respect to your files.

01Information We Collect

GDrive To Sheet does not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal files or Google Drive contents.

The product operates directly within the user's own Google Account through Google Apps Script and Google Sheets.

We do not operate any external processing servers for file analysis.

02Google Drive Access

The product requires Google permissions solely to:

  • Read folder and file metadata
  • Generate folder structure exports
  • Create spreadsheet outputs within the user's Google account

The tool does not upload file contents to any external server. All processing happens within your Google account.

03Data Storage

All generated data remains within the user's Google account.

We do not maintain a database containing exported file structures or any user-generated content.

04Analytics

The website may use basic analytics tools to understand website traffic and improve the product experience. Such analytics may collect:

  • Browser type
  • Device type
  • Country or region
  • Anonymous usage statistics

No Google Drive content is collected through analytics.

05Third-Party Services

This website may use the following third-party services, each of which has its own privacy policy:

  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting
  • Google Services — Google Sheets and Google Apps Script

06Security

We take reasonable measures to protect website security. However, no online service can guarantee absolute security.

07Children's Privacy

This product is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

08Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes become effective immediately upon publication on this page.

We encourage you to review this page from time to time for the latest information.
