First-Time Setup

Google requires a one-time authorization before your first export.

Open the Drive_Structure Sheet

Inside your copied spreadsheet:

  1. Open the Drive_Structure sheet
  2. Paste a Google Drive URL into cell B2
  3. Click Export Structure
2

Confirm Authorization

⚡ One-time setup — only 5 clicks

After clicking Export Structure, Google displays an authorization popup.

Click:

  1. OK
3

Security Warning

Google may display a security warning because the script has not completed Google's public verification process.

Click:

  1. Advanced
  2. Go to GDrive To Sheet (unsafe)
🔒 No file contents are uploaded to external servers.
4

Access Request

Grant the required permissions for Google Drive metadata access and spreadsheet output.

Click:

  1. Select all
  2. Continue
🚀 After clicking Continue, wait approximately 5 seconds.
The export process will start automatically without additional actions.
